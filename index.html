Hi! My name is imawardlysoc aka Alicia Huynh. I was born and raised in the Bay Area my whole life. I enjoy listening to music such as alternative rock/pop punk, reading books, and writing in my free time. I also like creating an ungodly amount of Spotify playlists.

I came up with the name imawkwardlysoc in eighth grade for apps that require me to have a username. I didn't want to expose my whole name.

I got into Digital Media Art in my senior year of high school. I originally was planning on majoring in computer engineering but liked the Digital Media world even more.
